About us

Zaqzouq Muay Thai started with a vision to provide authentic and high quality Muay Thai training in Jordan for people of all levels. Now, the team is home to amateurs and professional athletes in the area who come for a variety of reasons.Experienced in fitness, combat, fitness enthusiasts, military to fighters and professional athletes who come to improve technique, prepare for international tournaments, and footwear to advance their careers.
